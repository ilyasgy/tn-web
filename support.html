2. What to Include

To resolve issues quickly, include the following in your message:

  • your website domain;
  • the service you purchased;
  • the email you used at checkout;
  • a short description of the issue or question.

3. Typical Response Time

We usually respond within 24–48 hours. If we need more information to confirm scope or access, we will ask before continuing.

4. Report Questions

If you have questions about your report findings, reply to the same email thread where you received the report. This helps us keep context and respond faster.

5. Scope Changes

If you want to add new domains, new flows, or expand scope after purchasing, contact us first. Additional scope may require a separate quote or timeline.
